Gonococcal Infection, Treatment

Scalp abscess or disseminated gonococcal infection: 25 mg/kg/dose IV/IM every 12 hours for 7 days

Note: Dosing is significantly lower than other contemporary dosages; use clinical judgment.

Ophthalmia neonatorum: 100 mg/kg IV/IM as a single dose

Note: Only recommended if ceftriaxone is contraindicated.

Meningitis

Duration of therapy depends on the pathogen and clinical response:

  • Gram-negative meningitis: ≥21 days and ≥14 days after first negative CSF culture
  • Gonococcal meningitis: 10–14 days
